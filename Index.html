Jasper Epperson

Nacho Libre is about a man who works for a mexican monastery where many orphans live. Nacho and a homeless man (Esqueleto) that he became friends with start to have Lucha Libre matches where they constantly lose. Nacho and Esqueleto have a falling out and now they fight each other in the battle royale, who ever wins the battle gets to fight the Lucha champion, Ramses. Nacho finishes second and loses but to do an injury by the winner, Nacho gets to fight. If he wins, he will have enough money to get all of the orphans out of the bad conditions. He is nearly pinned in the fight until he sees the orphans and his eagle powers are activated and he comes back and pins Ramses for his first win in his Lucha Libre career.

Major Charecters

Nacho is an orphan who earns money from Lucha matches so that he can save up enough money so he can take the orphans and give them a good life.

Esquelto starts out as a homeless man until Nacho has an encounter with him and offers him to be his partner in their new Lucha Libre career.

Ramses is Nacho's inspiration but turns out to be bad.
